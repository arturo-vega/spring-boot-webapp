About Us

Our Story

The Pool Supply Depot was created out of the love of swimming and the joys that a backyard pool brings to families and communities. Founded in 2023 we embarked on a journey to be the best pool supply store on the web. Our team is composed of pool enthusiasts and experts. We know the nuances of pool ownership and maintenance. We are more than a business, we are a community dedicated to enhancing your pool experience.

Our Mission

At the Pool Supply Depot we have one simple goal: to provide top-quality pool supplies and great service, ensuring that maintaining your pool is as enjoyable as swimming in it. We strive to be your go-to source for everything from pool chemicals to cleaning equipment and more.
